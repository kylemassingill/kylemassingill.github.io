About the Series

The Astro Water Vapor seminar was started for two main reasons. The first is we noticed a lot of astronomy teams were independently working on similar atmospheric mitigations strategies. We thought a seminar series would be a great way for groups to compare notes and reduce the duplication of effort. The second reason was to create the opportunity for interdisciplinary collaboration between astronomers and remote sensing/atmospheric experts.
